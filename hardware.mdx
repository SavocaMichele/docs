## Installed hardware

- **CPU **\- AMD Ryzen™ 9 7950X | 16 / 32 Cores / Threads @ 4.5GHz - 5.7GHz
- **RAM **\- 32GB CL30 DDR5 6000MHz
- **GPU **\- Integrated
- **Storage **\- 2TB M.2 NVMe SSD PCIe 4.0 @ Read 7300MB/s Write 6600MB/s SLC-Cached
- **Network **\- 2.5GbE LAN @ Download 1Gbit Upload 60Mbit
- **BIOS **\- 256Mb Flash ROM, UEFI AMI BIOS
